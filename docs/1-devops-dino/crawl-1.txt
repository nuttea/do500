Exercise 1
The Manual Menace

Turmoil has engulfed the Infrastructure Republic. The manual configuration of applications in outlying cluster systems is in dispute.


Hoping to resolve the matter with a blockade of deadly manual hotfixes, the greedy  Manual Federation has broken all configuration changes on the small planet of Kubernetes.

While the Congress of the Republic endlessly debates this alarming chain of events, the Supreme Chancellor-as-Code, has secretly dispatched two Jedi Knights, Ansible & OpenShift, the guardians of automation and regression in the galaxy, to settle the conflict....
